Virat Kohli - ODI Stats

Virat Kohli: The Legend of ODI Cricket

Virat Kohli is an Indian cricketer and former captain of the Indian national cricket team. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest batsmen in the history of One Day International (ODI) cricket.

As of February 2025, Virat Kohli has played 296 One Day Internationals (ODIs) for India. In 284 innings, he has amassed 13,911 runs at an impressive average of 57.96 and a strike rate of 93.52.

His highest score in ODIs is 183. Throughout his ODI career, Kohli has achieved 50 centuries and 72 half-centuries, hitting 1,303 fours and 151 sixes.

In the field, he has taken 154 catches and holds several records, including being the fastest to reach multiple run milestones in ODI history.

Notable Records

  • Fastest to 8,000, 9,000, 10,000, 11,000, 12,000, and 13,000 runs
  • Most centuries in chases
  • 50 ODI centuries
  • Consistent ODI batting average above 50
